Two Categories of Issues

Through the trial, two categories became clear: service-side improvement issues and YKK's fundamental organizational challenges.

A

AI Reflection (Service) Issues

Based on field feedback, these are areas where our service needs improvement.

B

YKK's Fundamental Organizational Challenges

These are organizational challenges unique to YKK that surfaced through the trial. We believe AI Reflection can contribute to solving them.

Individuals lack clear goal-setting (HR goals are set during annual performance reviews with supervisors), resulting in weak awareness of daily actions and growth tied to objectives. This context manifests as the following four challenges.

Y-1

Disconnect between corporate strategy and individual goals

Mid-term management plans and business strategies are not cascaded from division to team to individual action goals. Goals set during annual HR reviews are abstract and do not function as daily behavioral guidelines.

Y-2

Workplace silos and insufficient dialogue

The "one-person master" structure in engineering departments and shift work at manufacturing sites (1,500 employees) create a lack of context-sharing between supervisors and subordinates. Opportunities for dialogue about goals and growth are structurally insufficient.

Y-3

Dilution of corporate DNA

The average age at the Kurobe Mother Factory is 37. With a younger workforce and increasing mid-career and diverse hires, YKK's 100-year history and founder Tadao Yoshida's philosophy are no longer naturally absorbed.

Y-4

Tacit knowledge personalization and attrition risk

The know-how of skilled manufacturing and sales talent remains locked within individuals, at risk of being lost through resignation or transfer. Without goal records or growth documentation, handover is extremely difficult.

Conclusion: By improving service issues and upgrading the product to address YKK's fundamental organizational challenges, we will deliver true value.

Proposal: Upgrade to V1.5

Evolving the product based on trial insights and YKK field feedback

We continuously incorporate YKK field feedback into our product evolution. V1.5 is an evolved version that simultaneously achieves service issue improvements and addresses YKK's fundamental challenges.

CategoryDetails
Service improvementReduced dialogue time (S-1), shift to critical thinking (S-2), goal alignment (S-3)
Addressing fundamental challengesStrategy-to-individual goal consistency (Y-1), automated DNA dissemination (Y-3), tacit knowledge formalization (Y-4)
01
CONTEXT INJECTION

YKK DNA Injection

Train the AI on YKK's history, YKK Spirit, management philosophy, core values, and mid-term management plan.

EffectAI engages through "YKK standards." Instead of excessive praise, it promotes critical thinking
UX change"Praised for the obvious" → "By YKK standards, shouldn't you think further?"
02
AI-DRIVEN ALIGNMENT

AI-Assisted Personal Goal Setting

Pre-load team policies and departmental goals into the AI, which then supports individual goal-setting through dialogue.

EffectEnsures consistency from corporate strategy to individual goals. "What to aim for" becomes clear
UX change"Abstract slogans" → "Specific action goals"
03
DAILY COACHING

Goal-Aligned Daily Check-in

Link personal goals to daily check-ins. AI asks questions aligned with each individual's objectives.

Effect"Aimless reflection" → "Course correction toward goal achievement"
Time20–30 min → 5–10 min (unnecessary questions drastically reduced)
